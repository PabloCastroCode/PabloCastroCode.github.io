Pool Ladder Stopper

Our pool ladder kept on scraping the wall because our rubber stoppers would shrink and fall off. So, I 3D printed a different version to act as a more permanent fix.

Spring-powered Butterfly and Heart

I saw a video of rubber-band-powered butterflies flying out of a book. Trying to replicate it, I designed 3D-printed, rubber-band powered spinning hearts. Using what I learned, I traced a monarch butterfly on Fusion and combined it with the same spring mechanism to bring it to life.

Piranha Plant Drain Cleaner

I 3D-Printed a drain cleaner to clean a shower in my house. As I used it, I thought it looked like a piranha plant so I designed a new one that did. I even made the stand a pipe so it looks like the video game.

Desk Organizer

My first 3D-printed project was a universal desk leveler. After several prototypes, we realized we were solving a problem that could be fixed just by loosening a screw or slipping in a piece of paper, so we scrapped it. Still, the process taught me to enjoy researching, collecting data, and applying it toward solutions.

Leaf Net

Because of expensive landscaping fees, communal discontent, and lack of municipal help, banyan trees and their leaves created a big issue in my neighborhood. So, I decided on making a product that would solve it. I came up with a net that has a hole in the center which when lifts up and acts as a funnel into a garbage bag. This tackled the problem of collection. By adding grass clippings and water I transform them into fertilizer. But this process of disposal is difficult as it requires a lot of space to store the bags while they ferment.
